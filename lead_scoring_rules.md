# Sales Lead Scoring Rules

- Visible need signal: 20
- Public and appropriate contact path: 15
- Match with existing proof: 20
- Plausible willingness to pay: 15
- Small pilot can ship in 3-10 days: 15
- Warm/referral access: 10
- Risk adjustment: 5

Categories: A 85-100, B 70-84, C 50-69, D below 50. A score is prioritization guidance, not proof that a company needs or wants the proposed software.
