# Email Sender Setup

Use a professional domain mailbox with sender name `Deepan Karthick`. Configure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC before scaling. Start with at most five manually reviewed, highly personalized messages per day. Record bounces and opt-outs immediately; never follow up after an opt-out. Avoid open-tracking pixels for the first experiment. Keep a plain-text signature with name, portfolio, city, and a reply-based opt-out.

No email in the queue is approved or sent by the dashboard automatically.
